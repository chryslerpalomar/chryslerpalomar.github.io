OVERVIEW

My journey through studying the course 'CPE 232 - Managing Enterprise Servers' was fun. and exciting.

In the week 1 to 6, we have studied the DevOps culture in general. We installed git, set up CentOS, and then we also performed and studied how to use SSH and set up an ansible environment then lastly, to implement roles.

In weeks 7 to 12, we begin managing files and creating roles by utilizing Ansible. We installed services and performed tasks with groups via the Ansible Playbook. Like ElasticStack, MariaDB, Apache2, Nagios, Prometheus, etc.

In weeks 13 to 18, Docker was introduced to us, and then we set up a cloud operating system on our machines, the OpenStack. Throughout the final weeks, we were installing OpenStack services such as the Keystone, Glance, Nova, Neutron, Horizon, etc.
